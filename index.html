Format Rulebook
Golf Formats
  • 2-Man Scramble: Both partners tee off, select the best shot, and both play from that spot until holed.
  • Texas Scramble: All players tee off and play from the best shot; a minimum number of drives from each player must be used.
  • Best Ball: Each player plays their own ball; only the lowest individual score among teammates counts.
  • Modified Chapman: Both partners tee off, switch balls for second shots, then select the best and play alternate shot until holed.
  • Singles: Two players compete head-to-head, each playing their own ball.
Handicapping
  • 2-Man Scramble: 35% of low handicap + 15% of high handicap
  • Texas Scramble: 50% of handicap, no strokes on Par 3s
  • Best Ball: 75% of handicap
  • Modified Chapman: 50% of low + 30% of high, none on Par 3s
  • Singles: 75% of handicap
  • Team Scramble: No strokes
Other Rules
  • Each match is worth 1 point. 1 pt win · 0.5 tie · 0 loss.
  • Two 2-man teams selected for each 9-hole match.
  • No player can miss three consecutive games.
  • 5th man from each team plays as a twosome — free to gamble, no points awarded.
  • All players will play in singles matches.
  • Team pairings announced the night before · one substitution per day.
